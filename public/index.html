Hi, I’m Muhammad Arslan Iqbal 👋

PhD Economics candidate focusing on behavioral & experimental economics.

I’m a PhD Economics candidate at the University of Melbourne. I study behavioral and experimental economics with a focus on leadership under weak institutional frameworks for accountability. I design lab experiments to test mechanisms that encourage responsible leadership and welfare-optimizing reforms.
